What is MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a widely used personality assessment tool that categorizes individuals into 16 distinct personality types based on four key preferences:

Extraversion vs Introversion

Where you direct your energy

Sensing vs iNtuition

How you gather information

Thinking vs Feeling

How you make decisions

Judging vs Perceiving

How you approach life
